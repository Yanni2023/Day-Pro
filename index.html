Code x is a Non-Profit Breaking Minorties Into Tech!

Our vision is to empower and inspire minorities to break into the tech industry by providing them with the resources, mentorship, and education they need to thrive.
Through our initiatives, we strive to create a supportive and inclusive environment that fosters growth, creativity, and collaboration.
Our mission is to bridge the gap between aspiring minority software engineers and the resources they need to succeed.
Join us in our vision to create a world where diversity, equity, and inclusion are at the forefront of the tech industry, and where individuals from all backgrounds have the opportunity to pursue their dreams and make a positive impact in the world.

01

Access To Education & Training

Our initiative can provide access to resources, training programs, and mentorship opportunities to help individuals develop the skills they need to succeed.

02

Networking Opportunities

We provide networking opportunities to help individuals connect with other professionals in the industry, learn about job opportunities, and find mentors who can offer guidance and support.

03

Career Development Support

We can provide career development support, such as resume building workshops, interview coaching, and career counseling to help individuals navigate the job market and succeed in their careers.

Code X Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to help underrepresented minorities. Our curriculum covers a range of topics, including programming languages, data analysis, web development, and software engineering. Whether you're just starting out or are looking to advance your career in tech, our curriculum can provide you with the resources and support you need to succeed. So don't wait - check out our curriculum today and start your journey towards a career in tech!
